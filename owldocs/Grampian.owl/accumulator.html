Accumulator

Farmers who are expansionist and business oriented, seeing their farm primarily as a set of resources which may be redeployed to gain the greatest profit. They may be prepared to change the farm structure fundamentally in response to changing opportunities, perhaps even to inclue non-agricultural activities. Their defining characteristic is a willingness to depart from set ways and to take risks. (Shucksmith 1993, p. 469)
